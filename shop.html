Buying Online

- I use Amazon Handmade to host my web shop, the links below will take you to each product listing.
- You can use credit/debit/Amex card with your Amazon existing account with full buyer protection.
- To pay cash & collect from the London studio, please contact me me to arrange. In this instance I can also offer 10% discount!
- Items will always be shipped tracked Royal Mail, or DPD/UPS. I will never use Hermes, Yodel or other low cost/high risk carrier.
- I am a big believer in accessible art. If you are on low income, please contact me to discuss making my work more affordable for you.
