Foul Play

Whilst experimenting with some new shapes and ideas on the wheel, I started making what looked like closed form bowling pins. Inspired by the 10-time Oscar winning theatrical masterpiece, Chicken Run (especially the chunkier hens, like Bunty and Edwina, they struck me as shapes that would be perfect for some feathered friends.

I've been throwing the chickens for a while now, each with a quirk and personality of their own. Be it a Wild West tall hat, or a pearl necklace, a la Marge Simpson, I like to make them friendly and characterful. Have a browse at my hen's gallery below and tell me your favourite?

Oh, and there's also eggs! Some with more surrealism than others...
